About Me

I don't fit one box.
I never have.

With over a decade of experience across IELTS coaching, learning program design, nonprofit leadership, and personal development, I've built a career around one core belief: people grow when given the right tools, the right environment, and someone who genuinely believes in their capacity.

I combine sociological insight with a coach's empathy and a writer's clarity. Whether working with an IELTS candidate, a nonprofit team, or a professional navigating a life transition — I bring the same rigour, warmth, and commitment to results.
